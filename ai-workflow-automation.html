AI Workflow Automation

Stop doing the work software should do. Connect your tools, automate repetitive tasks, and let your team focus on what actually matters.

What is workflow automation?

Every business runs on repetitive processes. Incoming emails that need sorting. Documents that need approval. CRM records that need updating. Invoices that need sending. These tasks don't require human judgment — they just eat human time.

AI workflow automation connects your existing tools and handles these tasks automatically. It watches for triggers (an email arrives, a form is submitted, a payment is received) and runs the appropriate actions without anyone lifting a finger.

What we build

We build custom automation systems tailored to your specific operations. Here's what that looks like in practice:

  • Email & document automation — auto-sort incoming mail, extract key data, generate responses, file documents to the right folders
  • CRM pipeline automation — auto-create contacts, update stages, log activities, send follow-ups based on behavior
  • Approval workflows — route purchase orders, leave requests, or contracts to the right person with automated reminders and escalation
  • Notification & alert systems — real-time alerts on Slack, WhatsApp, or Telegram when specific events occur
  • Cross-tool sync — keep data consistent between your CRM, accounting software, email platform, and project management tools

Who needs this

If your team spends more than a few hours a week on tasks that follow a predictable pattern, you're a candidate. Common triggers we hear:

  • "We manually enter every lead from our website into the CRM"
  • "Someone has to forward emails to the right department every morning"
  • "Invoices are created by hand and sent one at a time"
  • "We use three tools that don't talk to each other"

How it works

1. Audit. We map your current processes and identify where automation has the highest impact.

2. Build. We develop and test the system in your environment, connecting your tools through APIs.

3. Deploy. The system goes live. We monitor it and make adjustments as needed.

4. Support. Ongoing maintenance and updates as your business evolves.
