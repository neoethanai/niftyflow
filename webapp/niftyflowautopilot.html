Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting the free trial.

How long does setup take? +

Setup is a one-time process. You need: (1) Active Zerodha account, (2) F&O section enabled, (3) API key created and authorized, (4) Telegram account setup for notifications. Once all are ready, we configure NiftyFlow AutoPilot and you're set to start trading. The entire process typically takes a few hours to complete all prerequisites.

Can I see trades in trial period? +

Yes. During the 3-month free trial, you'll see every single trade in real-time in your Zerodha account. You observe performance, drawdowns, everything.. Then decide if it's for you.

What if I have a losing month? +

Losing months happen. Even the best systems have them. We show you worst-case scenarios upfront. Your job: trust the system during drawdowns. That's where discipline separates winners from quitters.

Do I need trading experience? +

Not really, But it is always if you understand basic options concepts (calls, puts, premiums). If you're completely new to options, spend 2-3 hours on YouTube learning basics .. This system handles execution, not education.

What if I don't like it after 1 month? +

You can pause or cancel after month 1. No penalties. We understand not every system works for everyone. The free trial is specifically designed so you can test risk-free.

Can I withdraw money during the trial? +

Yes. Your money is always yours. But for accurate trial results, we recommend keeping capital intact for 3 months so the system can execute uninterrupted. You can withdraw afterward.

What if you go out of business? +

Your funds are always in your Zerodha account, never with us. If NiftyFlow shuts down, your money doesn't disappear. You simply stop receiving automated signals. That's why full control matters.

Will this work with my current capital? +

We recommend ₹1,00,000 - ₹5,00,000 as the ideal range. Less capital limits position sizing. More capital means larger absolute profits but need a bigger risk appetite. Contact us about your specific capital.

Is this better than manual trading? +

Manual trading gives you freedom but requires discipline. AutoPilot gives you discipline automatically. Most traders lose money because of emotions - over-trading, revenge trading, FOMO. AutoPilot eliminates that. You'll have inconsistency if emotions guide you; consistency if rules guide you.

How often do trades happen? +

Depending on market conditions. The system is always in market hence you will always have 1 trade running. Consistency is key.

Can I use this with other trading? +

We recommend against mixing NiftyFlow with manual trading. The system is designed to work standalone. If you're doing your own trades too, you might sabotage the discipline that makes AutoPilot work.
