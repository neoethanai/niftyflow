Custom AI Chatbots

Conversational AI systems for customer support, lead generation, and internal operations. Deployed on Telegram, WhatsApp, or embedded on your website.

What we build

We build AI-powered chatbots that understand natural language, integrate with your backend systems, and handle real tasks — not just scripted FAQ trees. These are custom-built agents that talk to your databases, process orders, qualify leads, and escalate to humans when needed.

  • Customer support bots — answer questions, look up orders/accounts, handle returns, escalate complex issues to your team
  • Lead qualification bots — ask qualifying questions, capture contact details, push warm leads to your CRM automatically
  • Internal operations bots — approve requests, check inventory, pull reports, trigger workflows from a chat interface
  • Multi-agent systems — one bot that routes between specialized sub-agents depending on the request (support, sales, billing)

Where they run

We deploy on whichever platform your customers and team already use:

  • Telegram — best for global reach, rich API, no approval process, full automation capabilities
  • WhatsApp — best for Indian market, high penetration, familiar to most users
  • Website embed — best for capturing visitors on your site before they leave

Built example: Personal AI Finance Agent

A conversational AI assistant on Telegram that tracks income and expenses, categorizes transactions, and answers natural-language queries. Built and running on our own account. See it in our portfolio →

How it works

1. Scope. We identify what the bot needs to handle — support tickets, lead capture, order processing, or all of the above.

2. Build. We develop the bot, connect it to your existing systems via API, and train it on your data.

3. Deploy. The bot goes live on your chosen platform. Users start interacting immediately.

4. Refine. We monitor conversations, improve responses, and add features as your business grows.
