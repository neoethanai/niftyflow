Comprehensive AI Services

Seven areas, each solving a distinct set of operational problems. Built for teams ready to move fast, cut waste, and win bigger with AI.

AI Workflow Automation

Eliminate repetitive manual work across your operations.

  • Email automation — intelligent monitoring, priority classification, AI-drafted replies, and daily digests
  • Document processing — contracts, invoices, and reports automatically parsed, summarized, and routed
  • Internal workflow automation — approvals, task routing, notifications, and data entry handled by AI agents 24/7
  • CRM & pipeline automation — lead scoring, follow-up sequences, and deal tracking

AI Voice & Communication

Never miss a call or let a lead go cold.

  • AI voice agents — inbound and outbound call handling, appointment booking, and lead qualification
  • Call transcription & memory systems — every call recorded, transcribed, summarized, and action items extracted
  • AI receptionist — 24/7 call answering with natural conversation, call routing, and message taking
  • Voicemail-to-action — voicemails transcribed and fed to AI for immediate follow-up

Invoice & Financial Automation

Keep billing accurate and cash flow moving without manual effort.

  • Automated invoice generation — from deal data, timesheets, or project milestones
  • Payment tracking & reminders — overdue invoices chased automatically
  • Bookkeeping sync — transactions logged to QuickBooks, Xero, or custom systems
  • Financial reporting — AI-generated monthly summaries, cash flow analysis, and expense categorization

Customer Support & Engagement

Customers get answers instantly, at any hour.

  • AI chatbots — website, WhatsApp, Telegram, and SMS for FAQs, booking, and support tickets
  • Knowledge base agents — AI trained on your company documents to answer questions instantly
  • Ticket triage & routing — incoming support requests classified and assigned automatically
  • Sentiment analysis — monitor customer feedback across channels in real time

Lead Generation & Marketing Automation

Scale outreach and content without scaling headcount.

  • AI lead generation — automated prospecting, enrichment, and scoring
  • Cold outreach automation — AI-personalized email and LinkedIn sequences at scale
  • SEO content generation — blog posts, landing pages, and product descriptions optimized for search
  • Social media automation — AI-generated posts, scheduling, engagement monitoring, and trend analysis
  • Reputation management — AI monitors reviews, alerts on negatives, and drafts responses
  • Competitor monitoring — tracks competitor pricing, content, and positioning changes

Custom Software & AI Development

Purpose-built systems that fit your workflow, not the other way around.

  • Custom AI agents — built for your specific business processes
  • Full-stack web and mobile applications with AI integration
  • API integrations — connect any tools in your stack (CRM, ERP, email, calendars, databases, payments)
  • Data pipelines — ETL, data warehousing, and analytics dashboards
  • RAG systems — AI that answers questions from your private documents and knowledge bases

AI Infrastructure & Deployment

Secure, private, and production-ready AI systems.

  • Local and private AI deployment — models running on your own hardware, zero cloud dependency
  • Hardware configuration — Mac Mini, GPU servers, and NAS setups optimized for AI workloads
  • VPS & cloud infrastructure — architecture design, provider selection, and vendor-negotiated pricing
  • AI security — prompt injection protection, access controls, audit logging, and data isolation
  • Multi-agent orchestration — complex systems with multiple AI agents collaborating on business processes
